Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our string art services

Our typical turnaround time is 7-14 business days from order confirmation. Complex designs or larger pieces may take slightly longer. We'll provide an estimated completion date when you place your order.

High-resolution photos work best - at least 1000x1000 pixels. Photos with good contrast and clear details translate beautifully into string art. We can work with most photo formats, but we'll let you know if we need a better quality image.

Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. We're working on expanding to international shipping. Contact us if you're outside these areas - we may be able to make special arrangements.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely happy with your string art, contact us within 30 days of delivery. We'll work with you to make it right, whether that's adjustments or a full refund.
